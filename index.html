Safety (all modes)
NEVER Damp/ZeroTorque while standing. Must StandDown first (FSM 4 → 1). Damping from standing drops the robot instantly.
Phantom walk (thermal throttle). After ~30 min, knee motors overheat (passive cooling only). MCU kills torque without updating FSM — robot still reports 501 but Move() does nothing. Only fix: power cycle. Run g1_thermal_monitor.py to detect early.
Never idle stand. Keep the robot moving or go to damping. The policy holds joints in high-torque positions when still, accelerating overheating.
Hip overheating: stiffness reduced to 75% (2026-04-02). Press F in terminal to check temps. If joints exceed 80°C, keep moving or damp.
Motors MUST get kp=0 kd=0 explicitly before recovery or they seize. Never skip zero-torque step in shutdown.
FSM 501 requires app authorization. Walk mode only enters correctly via the full wake sequence: Damp(1) → StandUp(4) → BalanceMode(0) → Walk(501).
Forward jump (ID 17): high risk — ensure large clearance and a spotter.
Dance clips: motions may include arm swings, kicks, and jumps. Give the robot space.
Global drift: SONIC tracks local motion only — the robot will drift from its start position over time.
